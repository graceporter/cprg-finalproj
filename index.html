Intro

Join me while I document my training progress for my first half-marathon! My page includes highlights from past runs, my plan for upcoming training, how I've been fueling myself, and some lifting as well. Enjoy!

Where I like to Run

Griffith Woods

Griffith Woods is a park in the south west of Calgary. There is a perfect 5km loop here, which consists of dirt and paved trails. 45 Discovery Ridge Link S.W.

Glenmore Reservoir

Glenmore Reservoir is very close to home, and has an awesome 14km loop with varying elevations, all on a paved path. Lots to see, so it doesn't get boring! I start the run here: 1600 90 Ave SW, Calgary, AB

About My Training

So far, I've been trying to get in 2 shorter & faster runs to work on my pace time, and 1 long run a week. On top of that I have soccer once a week which is great for interval training.

Typically i lift to build muscle, but right now I'm just concerned about maintaing, as well as injury prevention. My current split is:

  • Push: chest, shoulders, triceps
  • Heavy legs: RDLs, KAS glute bridges, split squats, step ups, glute extensions, leg extensions. (Not all of these, I usually pick 3)
  • Pull: back and biceps
  • Light legs/stretching: This usually consists of light leg workouts to break up some of the lactic acid, and then a LOT of stretching & hip strengthening.
